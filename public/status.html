How to read this

A feed is fresh within its window (30 min; the VPS cron refreshes every 15). Stale means the last good snapshot is older than the window — the read plane keeps serving it rather than a worse one, and says so here. The gate degrades to ai_paused if the key is missing or expired, so the product never fabricates verdicts.
